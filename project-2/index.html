chicken tikka

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken breast or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoons ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • >1 teaspoon ground garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon ground chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt, to tast
  • 2 eggs

Steps

    For Cooking:

  1. 2 talespoons vegetable oil (if using a skillet)
  2. Lemon wedges, for serving
  3. Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish

    Prepare the Marinade

  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the yogurt, lemon juice, vegetable oil, minced garlic, grated ginger, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground turmeric, ground paprika, ground garam masala, ground chili powder, and salt. Mix well to create a smooth marinade. Marinate the Chicken:
  5. Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, ensuring each piece is well-coated. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or a lid and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. For best results, marinate overnight to allow the flavors to fully penetrate the chicken.

    6. Prepare for Cooking:

  6. If using an oven, preheat it to 400°F (200°C) and line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
    7. Place a wire rack on top of the baking sheet to allow the chicken to cook evenly.
  7. If using a grill, preheat the grill to medium-high heat and lightly oil the grates.
  8. If using a skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil over medium-high heat.

    9. Cook the Chicken:

  9. Oven Method:Arrange the marinated chicken pieces on the wire rack, leaving some space between each piece. Bake for 20-25 minutes, turning halfway through, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred around the edges.
  10. Grill Method Thread the chicken pieces onto skewers, if desired. Grill the chicken for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until cooked through and slightly charred.
  11. Skillet Method: Add the marinated chicken pieces to the hot skillet in a single layer. Cook for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and has a nice char. Serve:
  12. Transfer the cooked chicken tikka to a serving platter. Garnish with freshly chopped cilantro and serve with lemon wedges on the side. Chicken tikka is often enjoyed with naan bread or rice and a side of mint chutney or yogurt sauce.
